PREVENTIVE CARE

Preserve your natural structure and avoid tooth decay using digital checks and advanced hygiene protocols.

A healthy smile starts with strong preservation. At Dentysta, our preventive dentistry focuses on protecting your natural tooth enamel from disease, cavities, and structural recession.

We utilize high-end ultrasonic scaling to gently clear hardened plaque deposits without scratching tooth enamel. Combined with computerized cavity detection and low-radiation digital X-rays, our specialists catch potential tooth degradation months before standard visual scans.

Every session includes a comprehensive soft tissue check-up and an analysis of structural bone support to keep your teeth biologically integrated and structurally sound.

Your Preventive Session Timeline

Step 1: 3D Diagnostic Scan

We take digital intraoral scans and high-resolution imaging to evaluate structural enamel density and root integrity.

Step 2: Ultrasonic Bio-Scaling

Hardened plaque and staining are cleared using precision micro-ultrasonic scalers, followed by an organic polishing compound.

Step 3: Remineralization Coat

We apply a concentrated fluoride remineralizing varnish to strengthen enamel crystals and defend against acidic decay.

Step 4: Customized Maintenance Guide

Our specialists map a personalized daily maintenance routing plan customized to your specific enamel profile.
