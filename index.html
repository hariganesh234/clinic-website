REDEFINING CARE THROUGH PRECISION

At Dentysta, we combine biomimetic engineering with fully digital workflows to design smiles that are structurally stable, biologically integrated, and naturally beautiful. Our care philosophy focuses on minimally invasive protocols that keep your healthy tooth structure intact.

Biomimetic Dentistry

We restore teeth back to their natural strength and aesthetics using advanced adhesive materials and microscopes. By mimicking nature, we avoid aggressive reductions, saving up to 80% of your natural tooth structure.

3D Guided Surgical Planning

Every implant procedure is planned digitally using CBCT 3D scans combined with intraoral digital impressions. This allows us to print custom surgical guides for implant placement with sub-millimeter precision, reducing surgery times by 50% and speeding up recovery.

Computerized Local Anesthesia

We utilize The Wand®, a computer-controlled system that regulates the flow rate of anesthetic. This ensures that the injection is completely painless and targets only the specific tooth, eliminating the numbness of your entire face and visual needle fear.
