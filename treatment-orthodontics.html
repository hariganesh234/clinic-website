ORTHODONTICS

Align your teeth comfortably and virtually invisibly using advanced clear thermoplastic aligners.

A balanced bite improves long-term speech, digestion, and jaw joint health. We align teeth comfortably using high-end materials.

We utilize Invisalign clear aligners made from medical-grade, BPA-free SmartTrack thermoplastic. Our treatment planning starts with a 3D digital intraoral scan of your arches, simulating tooth movements step-by-step before your custom aligner trays are printed.

The clear aligner series applies gentle, calculated pressure to shift teeth systematically, giving you a beautiful alignment without metal brackets.

Your Orthodontic Progress Timeline

Step 1: 3D Digital impression

We scan your arches using intraoral lasers, building a digital model that charts the entire movement pathway from start to finish.

Step 2: Aligner Custom Fabrication

Your custom series of clear, ultra-thin thermoplastic aligners is fabricated in the laboratory to match the planned pathway.

Step 3: Transition & Check-ups

You wear each set for 22 hours daily, switching to the next series every 2 weeks, with clinic check-ups scheduled every 6 to 8 weeks.

Step 4: Alignment Stabilization

After your alignment target is reached, custom transparent retainers stabilize the bone fibers, securing your structural smile results.
